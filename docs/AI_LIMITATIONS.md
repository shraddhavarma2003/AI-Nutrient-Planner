# AI Nutrition System - Limitations & Disclaimers

## What This System Does ✅

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Nutritional Information** | Provides nutrition data from trusted databases (USDA, OpenFoodFacts) |
| **Safety Checks** | Evaluates foods against YOUR health profile |
| **Meal Suggestions** | Suggests healthier alternatives and fixes |
| **Pattern Tracking** | Identifies eating patterns and trends |
| **Exercise Estimates** | Calculates approximate exercise to burn calories |

## What This System Does NOT Do ❌

> ⚠️ **CRITICAL LIMITATIONS**

| Limitation | Explanation |
|------------|-------------|
| **No Diagnosis** | Cannot diagnose diseases or medical conditions |
| **No Prescriptions** | Cannot prescribe medications or treatments |
| **No Medical Advice** | Cannot replace professional medical consultation |
| **No Guarantees** | Cannot guarantee health outcomes |
| **No Emergency Care** | Not for medical emergencies (call 911/local emergency) |

## Data Sources

All nutrition data comes from verified sources:

- **USDA FoodData Central** - US Department of Agriculture
- **OpenFoodFacts** - Open-source food database
- **User-Verified Entries** - Manually checked against sources

We do NOT:
- Invent nutrition values
- Guess missing data
- Use unverified sources

## When to Seek Professional Help

If you experience ANY of the following, consult a healthcare provider:

- Unexpected symptoms or reactions
- Questions about medication interactions
- Eating disorder concerns
- Pregnancy or breastfeeding nutrition
- Chronic disease management
- Allergic reactions
- Significant weight changes

## Medical Safety Rules

This system enforces strict medical safety rules:

1. **Allergen Blocking** - Foods containing your allergens are BLOCKED
2. **Condition-Specific Limits** - Diabetics get sugar warnings, hypertensive users get sodium warnings
3. **No Override** - AI cannot override medical safety rules
4. **Human Review** - All rule changes require human approval

## Accuracy Disclaimer

While we strive for accuracy:

- Nutrition data may vary by brand, preparation method, and serving size
- User-entered data depends on input accuracy
- Calculations are estimates, not medical measurements
- Individual responses to foods vary

## Privacy & Data

- Medical profiles are encrypted
- Food logs retained for 90 days
- Chat history is session-only
- Uploaded images deleted after processing
- No data sold to third parties

For questions or concerns about this system, please contact the development team.
